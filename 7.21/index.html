Frequently Asked Questions

HOW DOES THE 14 DAY TRIAL WORK?

No credit card is required. At the end of the trial you will have the option to continue using Jobly by paying via credit card.

CAN I CANCEL MY PLAN ANYTIME?

Yes, there is no contract and no obligation. Jobly is a month to month service so use us when you need to hire and turn us off when you don’t.

HOW DO I INTEGRATE JOBLY INTO MY SITE?

We provide a URL that you can link to from your website (ex: jobs.jobly.io/companyname) so that you can get started hiring in minutes and don’t need to worry about hosting.

CAN I CHANGE FROM ONE PLAN TO ANOTHER?

Yes, change at any time and the new price will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

CAN I CHOOSE ANY PLAN FOR MY FREE TRIAL?

Absolutely! We are confident that you will love our product so we encourage you to use whichever plan is best for you. After your trial ends, you can easily upgrade or downgrade to other plans.

WHAT IF I GO OVER MY PLAN'S LIMITS?

You won’t be able to add more job postings than your plan allows so there is no need to worry about being over-billed.
